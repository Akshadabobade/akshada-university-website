Post Graduation

Savitribai Phule Pune University offers under graduate and post graduate degrees in more than 52 departments and institutes housed on and off campus. Please refer to the admissions section and contact each department individually for their specific requirements. Each Department has its own set of eligibility requirements depending on the specialization of the program/course offered. The eligibility requirements are also available at the Registrar's office or online at the University website, for information regarding conditions of eligibility for various programs and courses, please refer to the Savitribai Phule Pune University booklet (Circular No. 283, 2006-07) For queries, please contact PG Admissions Section, BOD, University of Pune.
